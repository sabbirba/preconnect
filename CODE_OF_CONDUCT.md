# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for all.

## Our Standards
- Be respectful and considerate in all interactions.
- Assume good intent and be open to feedback.
- Focus on constructive discussion and collaboration.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Disruptive or hostile behavior.

## Reporting
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please open a private report via GitHub Security Advisories.
